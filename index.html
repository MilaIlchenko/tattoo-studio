Shop Rules

Welcome! Please take a moment to go through our shop rules before entering. If you're bringing friends, kindly make sure they're aware of these guidelines.

  • No Photos, Please

    Please refrain from taking photos inside the shop. Not everyone may be comfortable having their pictures taken, especially during tattoo sessions. Let's respect everyone's privacy.

  • Limit to 1 Guest

    Due to our cozy space, we ask that you bring no more than one friend or family member with you during your session. This helps us maintain a comfortable environment for everyone.

  • Minimum Age

    18 Years Old: According to Danish law, you must be at least 18 years old to get a tattoo, and unfortunately, parental permission doesn't apply. Please bring a valid photo ID for age verification and registration.

  • No Animals Allowed

    For health reasons, we can't allow pets inside the shop. This is in compliance with the regulations set by the Health Authorities.

  • Your Belongings, Your Responsibility

    Remember to keep an eye on your belongings. We can't monitor your bags, coats, etc. Also, please be aware that we don't take responsibility for any clothing damaged during the tattoo process. Smoking, Alcohol, Drugs: Smoking is only permitted outside the shop. If you're under the influence of alcohol or drugs, we kindly ask you to leave the studio. Our focus is on providing a safe and comfortable experience for everyone.

  • Your Tattoo, Your Responsibility

    Before starting the tattoo process, carefully review and approve all aspects, including grammar, spelling, placement, and size. Once you give the green light, the artist won't be held responsible for these details. We want you to be 100% satisfied, so take your time to ensure everything looks just right.

Thank you for taking the time to read and follow our shop rules. We look forward to providing you with a positive tattoo experience!
